Game Modes

Classic Mode

Step into the nostalgia of the original snake game. Control the slithering creature and grow longer with each bite. Challenge your reflexes and strategy as you navigate the maze-like arena. Collect tasty treats, but beware of your own tail!

Timed Mode

Race against the clock in this adrenaline-pumping challenge! You have a mere 100 seconds to conquer the snake arena. To keep you on your toes, the delicious treats shift positions every 2 seconds.

Survival Mode

Embark on an intense survival journey where danger lurks at every turn. Dodge relentless enemies that chase your snake's head. A single collision will chip away at your score, but your snake will keep growing. The longer you become, the more foes arise!

Multiplayer Mode

Engage in intense Multiplayer battles. Navigate a shared arena with real players, each commanding their own snake.Hunt for food and seize power-ups tactically strewn across the battlefield. A TWIST AWAITS: while collisions are off the table, you can artfully glide through your snake's body. It's a heart-racing showdown of strategy and speed. Don't hold back—join the fray and claim supremacy now!




Legends

Orange represents the delectable food that fuels your journey. Consume these to grow longer and strengthen your snake's vitality.

The color blue signifies a unique power. Upon consumption, it grants your snake the ability to stun enemies, turning the tide of the game in your favor.

Yellow signifies a potent opportunity. Devour the yellow treats to witness your score double, propelling you towards greater achievements(Only in multiplayer mode)

Encounter the mystical aura of other-colored food. Ingesting these renders you invincible, shielded from danger for precious moments, as you navigate the challenges ahead (Only in multiplayer mode)
